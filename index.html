01 / The Operating System

I have built the system I run my company on.

Over the past year I've built an AI-native operating system for running my entire business: strategy, pipeline, content, coaching, daily operations. Not a dashboard. Not an app. A living architecture of 23 custom skills and plugins that evolves every week as I learn what actually works.

I use AI as a co-founder. My operating system sits across Gmail, Google Calendar, pipeline data, and content pipelines. It runs my morning check-in, reviews my pipeline against a $350K revenue target, drafts outreach in my voice, generates coaching proposals, publishes my newsletter, and flags when a deal is going quiet. All without me opening a spreadsheet.

The architecture matters: every skill reads from a single source of truth. Pipeline data flows into strategy reviews. Strategy reviews inform weekly planning. Weekly planning creates calendar blocks. Nothing exists in isolation. The system thinks in connections, not tasks.

It started as a 650-line PRD. Then an over-engineered four-plugin monster called Nebula. I killed that, stripped it back, and rebuilt it around a conversational skills architecture. Anthropic's skill-creator handles the testing and iteration loop. I design the skills, define the context, and the platform evolves the system alongside me.

Research Publication
Virginia AI Futures: The Fluency Question
Full-stack research publication. Scenario planning analysis of how AI fluency will reshape 780,000 Virginia businesses. Interactive assessment, live dashboard, regional analysis.
+
The problem
Virginia's business leaders needed a clear, data-grounded framework for understanding how AI would affect their businesses. Not hype. Not vendor pitches. Strategic analysis they could act on.

A five-chapter research publication using Shell's scenario planning methodology to construct four plausible futures for Virginia's SMB economy. Built as a full Next.js application with a designed reading experience: dark chapter headers, two-column body-plus-callout layouts, interactive regional analysis with gauge visualizations, a 2×2 scenario matrix, and a 10-question AI fluency assessment powered by Claude that generates personalised business analysis. Results stored in Neon Postgres for ongoing research. Mobile responsive. Live dashboard tracking 20 indicators across four scenarios.
